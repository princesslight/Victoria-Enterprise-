About Us

Victoria Enterprises Ltd is a Kampala-based company dedicated to bringing comfort to every home. We source and manufacture premium bedding materials using high-quality cotton, memory foam, and hypoallergenic fibers. Located in Industrial Area, Kampala, we serve homes, hotels, and hospitals across Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, durable, and comfortable bedding solutions that improve sleep quality and wellbeing for all Ugandans, while supporting local manufacturing and creating jobs.

Why Work With Us
